Chicken

Everyone knows I love me some amazing chicken. Here's a great recipe for some fried chicken!


Ingredients
  • Two pounds of chicken
  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour and other batter
  • Eggs

Directions
  1. Cut the chicken into even tenders
  2. Dip tenders in flour
  3. Dip chicken in egg wash
  4. Dip chicken back in flour
  5. Place chicken in fryer for 10 minutes at 300
