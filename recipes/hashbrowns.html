Hashbrowns

I've made these cheesy, amazing hashbrowns quite a few times. My kids love them and so do I!


Ingredients
  • Shredded potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Now that you have all the ingredients, let's get to making the hasbrowns

Directions
  1. Combine salt, pepper, shredded potatoes all into pan
  2. Place in oven for 10 minutes at 300
  3. Take out, mix eggs, add cheese on top. Place back in oven for 10 more minutes
  4. Take otu of even, let cool for 5 minutes. Enjoy!
